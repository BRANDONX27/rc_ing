MEXICO
The natural resources of Mexico are exceptionally rich, both in flora as in fauna; Mexico is cataloged as a among the countries with the highest biological diversity.

Mexico stands out worldwide in the production of minerals such as silver, copper, bismuth, arsenic, lead and zinc. Among its strengths we can mention the following:

1-First world producer of silver.
2-Tenth oil producer worldwide.
3-One of the first copper producing countries in the world
